Frequently Asked Questions

Here are things people ask us fairly often. If your question is not here, just call or use the form above.

How long does delivery take?

On most days, we deliver within 30 to 45 minutes from when your order is confirmed. It can take a bit longer during busy lunch hours or on weekends. We will always let you know if there is a delay before it happens.

Is there a minimum order amount?

There is no minimum order, but delivery is free only for orders above P100. For orders below that, there is a flat delivery fee of P20 regardless of how far you are within our zones.

Can I change or cancel my order after submitting it?

Yes, but you need to call us within 5 minutes of placing the order. Once we have started preparing your food, we cannot cancel it. Changes to quantities or items are possible up until preparation starts.

Do you cater for dietary requirements or allergies?

We do our best. If you have a specific allergy, mention it clearly in the order notes or call us before ordering. For serious allergies, a phone call is safer than the form because we can go through the ingredients with you directly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept card payments online, mobile money through Orange and Mascom, and cash on delivery. If you are paying with mobile money, we will send you the number to pay to after confirming your order.

Can I order for someone else at a different address?

Yes. Just put the recipient's address in the delivery address field and your own phone number so we can confirm the order with you. Leave a note telling us it is a gift if you want and we will sort out the rest.
