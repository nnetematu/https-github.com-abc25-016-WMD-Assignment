About Matux Kitchen

A Gaborone-born delivery service built on good food and honest values

Our Story

Established in 2022, what started as a dream became a reality built on passion, resilience and love for food. Matu Tlhase, a trained chef from Mochudi, had one idea, to make delicious, comforting meals accessible at the tap of a button.

Matu started small. She cooked out of her home kitchen in Mochudi, her brother did deliveries on a bicycle, and her cousin handled the phone orders. Within the first month they had 40 regular customers. By the end of that year, they had over 300.

Today Matux Kitchen operates as a fully online food delivery service, covering most areas in and around Gaborone. We have a proper kitchen, a team of riders, and a menu that has grown from 5 dishes to over 20. But the reason people order from us has not changed — the food is fresh, it tastes like it was made with care, and it shows up when we say it will.

How We Got Here

Our Journey So Far

2022

Started From Home

Amara launched Savana Kitchen from her kitchen in Mochudi with just 5 dishes with her brother handling deliveries and her cousin handling the phone orders.

2023

Moved to a Proper Kitchen

Growing demand meant we needed more space. We rented our first commercial kitchen in Gaborone CBD and brought on over 5 full-time staff members.

2023

Launched Our Website

We moved away from phone-only orders and launched our first website, making it easier for customers to browse the menu and place orders online at any time.

2024

Expanded Delivery Zones

We extended our delivery coverage to include Tlokweng, Mogoditshane, Phakalane and Extension 15, reaching thousands of new customers across the city.

2024

20+ Dishes and Growing

Our menu grew to over 20 items spanning starters, mains, desserts and drinks. We also introduced online payment options.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make it easy for people across Gaborone to eat well without the stress of cooking or going out. We want every order to feel like a proper meal — not fast food — delivered quickly and without fuss.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted food delivery service in Botswana, known for the quality of our food, the reliability of our service, and our commitment to helping the community in need as much as possible.

Our Promise

Every meal we deliver is cooked fresh on the day. We do not reheat yesterday's food or cut corners with cheap ingredients. If something is not right with your order, we will fix it — no arguments.
